About Me

About Me

I am Md. Mahbub Hasan from Bangladesh. I have received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh Army University of Engineering & Technology.

I started my professional career in LG Refrigerator Plant as an Assistant Engineer. During my service period, I worked in R&D and Factory Innovation and Development team. Currently, I am working in GREE Air Conditioner manufacturing plant. My core responsibilities include plant operation, development, machine installation, calibration, and maintenance.

Aside from professional life, I'm an enthusiast, optimist and diligent individual. I love to solve practical problems, travelling and conduct top-quality research.

I believe it's essential for every person to do what he loves and to love what he does. The thumb rule that I believe to achieve something is, “Think, Acquire, Apply”.
