About Me

I am Md. Mahbub Hasan from Bangladesh. I have received my BSc degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh Army University of Engineering & Technology.

Later on, I have worked in LG Refrigerator Plant as Assistant Engineer from June 2019 to April 2021. Currently, I am working in GREE Air Conditioner Plant. I monitor plant operation and make strategical decisions of this manufacturing industry.

Aside from professional life, I’m an enthusiast, optimist and diligent soul. I love to solve practical problems, travelling and conduct top-quality research. I believe it’s essential for every person to do what he loves and to love what he does.

The thumb rule, that I believe to be fundamental step to get something is, “Think, Acquire, Apply”.
